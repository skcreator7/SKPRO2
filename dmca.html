DMCA Notice

SK4FiLM complies with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) and responds to takedown notices in accordance with 17 U.S.C. § 512.

📌 Our Commitment

SK4FiLM does not host any copyrighted content on our servers. We only link to media files that are hosted on third-party platforms like Telegram. However, we respect intellectual property rights and will respond promptly to valid DMCA takedown requests.

📋 How to File a DMCA Takedown

If you believe that content available through SK4FiLM infringes your copyright, please submit a written notice containing the following information:

  • Identification: A physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or authorized representative.
  • Description: Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed.
  • Location: The URL or specific location of the infringing material on our site.
  • Contact: Your contact information (name, address, phone number, email).
  • Statement: A statement that you have a good faith belief that use is not authorized.
  • Statement: A statement that the information is accurate and, under penalty of perjury, you are authorized to act.

📧 Submit Takedown Request

Send your DMCA takedown notice to:
sk4film.official@gmail.com

⏱️ Response Time

We typically respond to valid DMCA notices within 24-48 hours. Upon receipt of a valid notice, we will remove or disable access to the allegedly infringing material.

🔄 Counter-Notification

If you believe that content was removed in error, you may submit a counter-notification with the following:

  • Identification of the material removed and its location before removal.
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief the material was removed in error.
  • Your name, address, phone number, and consent to jurisdiction.
  • Your electronic or physical signature.

Note: SK4FiLM is a search and indexing platform. We do not upload or host any copyrighted files. All content is linked from third-party sources.
